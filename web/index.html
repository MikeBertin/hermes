Hermes

Bitcoin from First Principles

Bitcoin, built from nothing — no crypto libraries, no magic. The secp256k1 curve, the hashes, the signatures, the proof of work, the network, all rebuilt by hand and turned into things you can touch. Fifteen interactive windows onto how a chain of numbers becomes money nobody can forge.
